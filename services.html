    Accessible & Inclusive Design

    Accessibility isn’t a checklist item or a final pass — it’s a mindset we bring into every stage of a project. We believe digital experiences should be usable, readable, and welcoming for as many people as possible.

    Our accessibility‑aware approach includes:

    • Clear content hierarchy and readable layouts
    • Thoughtful colour contrast and typography
    • Keyboard‑friendly and screen‑reader‑aware structures
    • Inclusive design decisions made from the start

      • By designing with accessibility in mind, we create experiences that are clearer, more resilient, and more human for everyone.
